Metric Definitions

Subscriber
A distinct person or organisation registered in the system. One subscriber can belong to multiple lists.
Subscription
A link between a subscriber and a specific mailing list. One subscriber on three lists counts as three subscriptions.
New Subscribers
Subscriber accounts created during the selected period.
New Subscriptions
New subscriber–list links established during the selected period (including re-activations).
Unsubscriptions
List opt-outs recorded during the selected period.
Active Subscribers
Distinct subscribers currently marked active who have at least one active list subscription.
Active Subscriptions
Total active subscriber–list links right now. This is the denominator used for percentages in the pie chart.
Inactive Subscribers
Subscribers whose account is marked inactive, or whose account is active but have no active list subscriptions.
Inactive Subscriptions
Subscriber–list links that have been opted out of, regardless of whether the subscriber account is active.
